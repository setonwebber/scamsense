This is a reimbursement scam! These types of scams will attempt to steal personal and bank account information.
The scammer will masquerade as a financial or government entity. Typically seen is the ATO, personal bank or utility provider.
A suspicious link will be provided for the victim to begin reimbursement/rebate proceeding; for which the form will ask for personal or bank account information.
Be careful as the message are intelligently crafted! Avoid any links sent via email or text message!

Scam Indictors:
1.	Suspicious Link
2.	Unsaved Number
